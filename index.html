The Suburban Life Ain't So Bad

I used to dream that after college I’d move out to California to live some kind of “modernista” lifestyle. Surfing. Beaches. Sunglasses. Perfect weather - every day. How could you not be happy if you had all that? It’s been nearly 2 months since the COVID-19 social distancing lockdown began. Living in New York City, the epicenter of the outbreak, has been enlightening to say the least. Sure, my feelings have changed as I get older, but I never realized how ungrateful i’d been until now.

May 8, 2020
